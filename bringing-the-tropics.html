Bringing the tropics to your taste buds - The Mango Story

Mangoes are not just a sweet & succulent fruit, but also a travel filled with unique experiences. From the orchards where they are grown to the markets where they are sold, the journey of mangoes is one that offers a fascinating insight into the world of agriculture and food production. Come, let’s explore the journey of mangoes from the farm to the table, as we reveal some of the secrets of this amazing fruit.

The Mango Harvest

The mango journey begins with the harvest. Mango trees produce fruit once a year, and it's the job of the farmers to pick the ripe fruit by hand. Mangoes are harvested when they are mature and sweet, which means that the timing of the harvest is critical to their flavour.

Sorting & Grading

Once the mangoes have been harvested, they are taken to the warehouse where sorting & grading are done. The fruit is classified based on its size, colour, and maturity. The sorting and grading process is crucial to ensure that only the best mangoes make it to the marketplace.

Packaging & Transport

After the mangoes have been sorted, they are packed and transported carefully to all the retail outlets. Mangoes are delicate fruit, and they require special care to prevent damage during transport. They are often transported in temperature-controlled environments to ensure that they arrive in perfect condition.

Arrival at the Retail Outlets

When the mangoes arrive at the retail stores, they are inspected again to ensure that they meet the required standards. Mangoes are sold in a variety of ways, including in bulk or pre-packaged. They are often displayed on trays or in baskets to entice customers with their bright colours and sweet aroma.

Consumption

Finally, the mangoes reach your table & you can enjoy this versatile fruit in a variety of ways. They can be eaten fresh, sliced, or used in a range of recipes, including smoothies, desserts, and savoury dishes.

The journey of mangoes is truly fascinating as it involves a lot of hard work, dedication, and care. Every step of the journey is critical, farmers & traders ensure that only the best mangoes make it to your tables. So, the next time you bite into a juicy mango, take a moment to appreciate the journey that brought it to you.
